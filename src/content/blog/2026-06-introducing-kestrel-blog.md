The blog system is now working and fully working with our CMS. If you’re looking for a simple way to manage blog content on a website, Decap CMS is worth checking out.

Posts can be created through our admin interface and will automatically appear on the site once published. We also have a basic author system in place to attribute posts properly.

This is the initial version of the setup. From here, the system will be expanded and refined as we figure out what works best.
